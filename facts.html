What is Swimming?

According to the dictionary, swimming is the act of propelling oneself through the water using the limbs.

What are the benefits of swimming?

  1. Low impact of your joints
  2. Burns many calories
  3. Builds muscle mass
  4. Increases lung capacity
  5. Any person of any age can swim

Fun facts

  • The slowest olympic stroke is the Breast Stroke
  • The fastest olympic stroke is the Front Crawl
  • The oldest swimming stroke is the Breast Stroke
  • More than 50% of swimmers have bad shoulders :)
  • Swimming became an olympic event in 1856
  • Over half the people in the US cant swim (are you one of them?)
  • Swimming buns more calories than running or biking
  • Benjamin Franklin invented swimming fins
  • The worlds oldest swimmer is 100 years old
  • The worlds youngest swimmer is 10 years old
  • Swimmers use nearly every muscle in their body
