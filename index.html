While many people know about the idea of swimming, there are many people who have no idea how to actually swim.
The point of the site is to bring swimming to the attention of people out there who would love to learn, but have not
time to book lessons. This site is intended for all adiences from older to younger, to be able to try to experiment on
their own in the water, and to help them understand the logistics of swimming.
